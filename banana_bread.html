Banana Bread

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup mixed nuts (walnuts, pecans, or almonds)
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup chopped almonds
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, sugar, and salt until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Be careful not to overmix.
  4. Gradually add the flour and brown sugar, beating until incorporated.
  5. Add the nuts and chopped walnuts, pecans, and almonds, and mix until just combined.
  6. Add the vanilla extract and mix until well combined.
  7. Pour the batter into prepared bread-making bowls, spacing them slightly apart.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  9. Cool the bread on a wire rack for at least 20 minutes before slicing.
  10. Enjoy your banana bread!
