Chicken Tortilla Soup

    Ingredients

  • 2 cups chicken breasts, skinned and cut into thin strips
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (16 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 (16 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

    Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and cook for about 30 seconds, until softened.
  3. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for about 15 minutes, until browned on all sides.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomatoes, Parmesan, mozzarella, and cheddar cheese. Cook for an additional 10 minutes, until the sauce thickens and becomes creamy.
  5. Serve the soup over tortillas.
